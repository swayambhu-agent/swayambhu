Mind Tab — Connected Graph View

Instead of separate panels, what if the Mind tab shows the cognitive flow as a connected graph?

A

Flow Graph — Left to Right

Experiences on the left flow into samskaras and desires in the middle, which flow into actions on the right. Lines connect them. Click any node to see details. The whole cognitive cycle is visible as a directed graph.

Experiences → S/D operators → Samskaras + Desires → Actions → new Experiences. The full cycle visible at once.

B

Hybrid — Graph + Panels

Top half: the flow graph (compact). Bottom half: detail panels that expand when you click a node. Best of both — you see the relationships AND can drill into specifics.
